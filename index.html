About

I'm a developer with over 5 years of experience developing software, and 3 years freelancing in games. My most recent work is the Android app for Croatia's largest classified ad website called Njuskalo, which I've worked on for more than 3 years during my employment at Undabot, one of Croatia's most prominent mobile development companies.

My latest success is developing a photo recognition feature that recognizes objects on the photos, where AI analyzes the photo and matches them with their belonging categories. If the AI has chosen the wrong category, the user can easily improve the AI by presenting the correct category while making the AI better and smarter.

I have also co-founded a small development company called quasiStudio, where we have created a couple of games, and currently work on a health & fitness assistant app called Nouri.

Njuskalo

App

Njuskalo is a leading classifieds Android and iOS app in Croatia, handling over 300,000 ads every month. It is so simple that users just keep on buying and selling.

While in Undabot, we have created fast and easy classifieds app ensuring simple, safe and intuitive interactions between buyers and sellers. Users can create and post ads in a matter of seconds.

An integrated messaging system which I have worked on keeps all of the selling and buying communication within the application. Users exchange over 210,000 messages every month, and are continuously informed about new posts through the notification system.

My latest success was developing a photo recognition feature that recognizes objects on the photos, where AI analyzes the photo and matches them with their belonging categories. If the AI has chosen the wrong category, the user can easily improve the AI by presenting the correct category.

Aero Attack

Game

Aero Attack is a retro shooter arcade game for iOS and Android where you play as a pilot named Bob, tasked to defend the human race from various aerial minions. It was our studio's debut game.

Fly Bob and his shooting plane through space, dodge the floating minions, alien bosses, satellites, and a couple of space cows. What makes it unique is the challenge of shooting the incoming minions, while keeping that nerve-wracking flappy style jump when avoiding them.

Aero Attack is an addictive game of discipline and skill, looking to make people feel nostalgic and sentimental, remembering the retro games everyone loved spending time on and emotions they stirred in us.

Nouri

App

Nouri is short of nourishment, which is the food that you need to grow and stay healthy. That’s what we want to be, and our goal is to help people do exactly that - grow and stay healthy.

Whether you’re looking to lose weight, tone up, get healthy, change your habits, or start a new diet, your assistant Nouri is here to help. It's a one-stop-shop for healthy living.

Users can connect their favorite apps to track what they consume, how much they exercise, while adding foods by scanning the barcode. Recipes can be searched by the ingredients users have available, and through our advanced photo-progress feature, users reach their goals.

We want to make it as easy as possible for anyone to get better insights into their health and food habits, providing guidance on how they can improve it. Nouri's release is planned for Q3-Q4 of 2020.

Wogibtswas

App

Wogibtswas is a leading geolocation shopping iOS and Android app in Austria. It notifies users about the best nearby discounts.

During my tenure at Undabot, we have built a smart shopping assistant covering more than 20,000 shops in Austria. The app connects users with shops in their vicinity by using real time location.

quasiStudio

Co-Founder

quasiStudio is a small, independent game studio founded by two tech guys who love games so much, and they couldn't help themselves but turn it into a full-time career. The goal is to create memorable digital experiences and have fun along the way.

Primary areas of work are mobile games for iOS and Android, but the quasi-team also knows its way around UI/UX, web and mobile app development.

During the starting phase, Kresimir and I worked from the comfort of our homes and needed a name under which we will publish the first game we've worked on. We couldn't be called a studio at that point, so we figured it's a quasi-studio anyways, which is how the business got its name.

Slizza

Game

Slizza is a fast-moving, action-puzzle arcade game for iOS and Android that you won't be able to drop out of your hands. Hundreds of single player levels, local or online multiplayer to play with friends, and daily challenges will ensure to deliver endless amount of fun.

Slizza is quasiStudio's second game. After the studio's first release, a retro space shooter called Aero Attack, Slizza was released for both iOS and Android on March 25th, 2019.
